## So What — Strategic Implications

- **Chinese humanoid IPO wave is real and accelerating.** Unitree approved (Jul 3), LimX pre-IPO closed (Jul 14), more expected. Public markets give these companies hard capital and Western investor attention — the fundraising velocity now matches or exceeds US peers. Intel's edge-robotics positioning needs a China-supply-chain counter-narrative beyond OpenVINO.

The AGLT ticker will create a public real-time performance barometer for humanoid RaaS unit economics — every future competitor will be measured against it.
