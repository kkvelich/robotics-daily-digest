## So what — strategic implications

- **Automate 2026 is next week's battleground.** NVIDIA's Humanoid Pavilion at McCormick Place signals a push to *own the deployment narrative* in front of 50,000 industrial decision-makers. Intel's hybrid-edge story needs visible presence and a credible customer story there — being undersized at Automate 2026 would be a visible gap.

- **Watch tonight's FF EAI livestream for silicon data point.** Faraday Future (NASDAQ: FFAI) is financially distressed but has shipped humanoid + quadruped products to early buyers. The June 16 event may name a compute partner — any landing on Qualcomm RB-series or MediaTek registers as competitive signal. If they name NVIDIA or Intel, it's a smaller-company validation story.

- **Hospitality is an emerging edge-compute beachhead.** HITEC shows service robots scaling in real deployments. Today it's commodity inference silicon. As voice-driven, multi-modal reasoning gets layered in (the Richtech + SoundHound voice-enabling partnership points in this direction), hospitality robots become NPU-class edge workloads — a market to develop partnerships in now.
