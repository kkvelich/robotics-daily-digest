If verifiable enterprise humanoid deployments remain narrow through 2026 (Figure AI + Agility, essentially), volume uptake of on-robot edge compute will lag consensus timelines. Intel's hybrid-edge narrative is structurally sound — but customer pipeline timelines need recalibrating downward for 2026.

2. **AGIBOT Forum today is live and worth monitoring.** Partnership or deployment announcements at the Embodied AI Forum (Jul 19) could shift the competitive framing for industrial humanoids in H2 2026. Check Pandaily and 36Kr for same-day drops.

3. **AGIBOT's double-edged export story.** Thai PM visit at WAIC signals real international demand for Chinese humanoids. TechTimes' China National Intelligence Law flag is the right counter-weight — AGIBOT's global export ambitions come with mandatory data-sharing obligations under PRC law. Intel-partnered system integrators should make this a standard diligence point when competing against AGIBOT in Western enterprise deals.

4. **AUTONOMOUS conference signals market maturation.** The inaugural AUTONOMOUS 2026 (San Francisco, Jul 16) drew practitioners, not researchers. Physical AI has crossed into commercial deployment conversations. Expect more practitioner-focused events in H2 2026 — and with them, more concrete OEM compute purchase decisions entering the pipeline.
